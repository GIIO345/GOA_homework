bmw

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German luxury automobile and motorcycle manufacturer headquartered in Munich, Germany. Founded in 1916, BMW originally produced aircraft engines before shifting to motorcycles and cars. It is known for its high-performance vehicles, innovative engineering, and premium quality. BMW owns brands like Mini and Rolls-Royce and is famous for its "Ultimate Driving Machine" slogan. The company produces a wide range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs (X series), sports cars (M series), and electric models (i series).

ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded in 1939 by Enzo Ferrari and officially established as Ferrari S.p.A. in 1947. Headquartered in Maranello, Italy, Ferrari is known for its high-performance, stylish, and exclusive cars, often associated with speed, racing, and prestige. The brand has a strong motorsport heritage, especially in Formula 1, where it holds numerous championships. Ferrari models, like the LaFerrari, 488, SF90 Stradale, and the iconic Ferrari F40, are renowned for their powerful engines, cutting-edge technology, and signature prancing horse logo.
